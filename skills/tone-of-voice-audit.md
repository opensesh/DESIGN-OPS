# Tone of Voice Audit

Audit content against brand voice guidelines, identify inconsistencies, and score adherence.

## Purpose

Evaluate written content for alignment with established brand voice and tone guidelines. Provide actionable feedback to improve consistency across communications.

## When to Activate

Use this skill when:
- Reviewing content before publication
- Auditing existing content for brand alignment
- Training team members on voice guidelines
- Evaluating vendor or freelance copy

---

## Process

### Step 1: Establish Voice Reference

If voice guidelines exist, reference them. If not, ask:
- "What are the core voice attributes? (e.g., friendly, professional, bold)"
- "Any words or phrases to always use or avoid?"
- "Who is the audience?"
- "What brand references should I use for comparison?"

### Step 2: Analyze Content

Evaluate against these dimensions:

**Voice Attributes**
- Does the content embody stated voice characteristics?
- Is the personality consistent throughout?

**Tone Appropriateness**
- Is the tone right for the context/channel?
- Does it match the emotional register expected?

**Language Patterns**
- Sentence structure (simple vs complex)
- Active vs passive voice usage
- Jargon and terminology choices

**Word Choice**
- On-brand vocabulary
- Avoided words/phrases
- Consistency in terminology

**Audience Alignment**
- Appropriate reading level
- Cultural sensitivity
- Inclusive language

## Scoring Rubric

**9-10:** Exemplary - Could be used as a brand voice reference
**7-8:** Strong - Minor adjustments needed
**5-6:** Acceptable - Several areas need attention
**3-4:** Weak - Significant revision required
**1-2:** Off-brand - Doesn't reflect brand voice

---

## Common Issues to Flag

**Voice inconsistency:**
- Shifts between formal and casual
- Personality that comes and goes
- Multiple writers' voices apparent

**Tone mismatches:**
- Too casual for the context (legal, crisis)
- Too formal for the channel (social)
- Emotional tone doesn't match content

**Language problems:**
- Passive voice overuse
- Unnecessarily complex sentences
- Jargon without explanation

**Word choice:**
- Off-brand terminology
- Inconsistent naming (product, feature names)
- Weak verbs, excessive adverbs

---

## Output Variants

**Quick audit (for fast review):**
- Score only
- Top 3 issues
- 3 revision examples

**Full audit (for important content):**
- Complete scoring breakdown
- All issues categorized
- Comprehensive recommendations

**Training audit (for team development):**
- Detailed explanations of why issues matter
- Multiple revision options per issue
- Reference back to guidelines
